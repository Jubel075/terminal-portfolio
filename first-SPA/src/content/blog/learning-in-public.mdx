For a long time I kept my unfinished work hidden. It felt safer. But the more
I shared, the faster I learned.

## Why share early

Feedback arrives sooner. You stop polishing things nobody asked for. And the
act of explaining a project forces you to understand it.

## What it taught me

- Done and shared beats perfect and private
- People are kinder than you expect
- Your past code is a progress bar, not an embarrassment

If you're learning too: post the thing. Future you will thank you.
