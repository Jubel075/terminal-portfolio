I started with a plain Vite + React starter and a vague idea: I wanted my
portfolio to feel like a terminal. Mono type, a blinking caret, a `$ whoami`
prompt — a developer's home directory you can scroll through.

## The direction

I explored three skins — editorial, terminal, and Swiss minimal — and landed
on the terminal one. It fits how I actually work, and the constraints (one
font, a tight palette) made the design decisions easier.

## The motion

Every section animates in once as it enters the viewport. The hero reveals
word by word, the role line types itself out, and the skills strip scrolls
forever. All of it is gated behind `prefers-reduced-motion`.

## What's next

More case studies, more writing, and maybe a real backend for the contact
form. For now, it ships.
