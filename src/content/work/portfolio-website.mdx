## Overview

A personal portfolio built to showcase projects, skills, and contact
information. The goal was a fast, accessible single-page app with a strong
point of view — a terminal aesthetic, all in JetBrains Mono.

## What I built

- A scroll-driven SPA with a scroll-spy navigation
- Motion-forward sections using Framer Motion
- Deep-linkable case studies and writing via react-router

## Stack

`React` · `Vite` · `Tailwind CSS` · `Framer Motion` · `react-router`

## Outcome

A site that loads fast, respects `prefers-reduced-motion`, and reads like a
developer's home directory.
